LuckyTools was born from a simple goal: to create the tools that professional editors actually want to use in their daily high-pressure workflows.

The Story Behind LuckyTools

LuckyTools was created after years of editing for top-tier creators and experiencing the friction of repetitive tasks firsthand. What began as a specialized project to automate professional workflows has evolved into a mission to empower editors everywhere.

What makes LuckyTools unique is its integrated ecosystem. It's not just a set of disconnected plugins—it's a harmonized environment where every tool works in sync to save you hours of manual labor.

The Power of Community

LuckyTools is developed in exclusive alliance with Busco Editor, the largest network of professional video editors in LATAM. We don't just build plugins; we are backed by an active community of 9,000+ elite creators who rigorously test and shape the new standard of editing. We are the ecosystem.
