LuckySearch

Your Central Hub for accessing everything from one place. Find tools, effects, presets, and more instantly.

  • Quick Access: LuckySearch appears and disappears on demand. Keep your workspace clean.
  • Customization: Change colors, fonts, or the search bar name to match your personal vibe.
  • Dynamic Shortcuts: Automatically surfaces your most recently used effects for instant re-application.
  • Zero-Lag: Enjoy real-time preset reading with absolutely zero lag.

LuckyShortcuts

Turbo Hotkeys designed to let you assign individual actions inside LuckyTools directly to your keyboard.

  • Zero Latency: Move faster when applying presets, transitions, or effects to clips.
  • Direct Actions: Lightning-fast, 1-to-1 shortcuts to essential actions.
  • Note: Complex multi-effect macros are not supported to guarantee absolute stability.

LuckyOrganize

Structure Folders instantly to keep your project bins perfectly organized and visually coherent.

  • Auto-Sort: Organize media and sequences based on predefined logical patterns.
  • Easy Setup: Set a folder name, color, and text filter—no overwhelming settings.
  • Note: Exclusively manages items within the Premiere Project panel.

LuckyZoom

Surgical Framing for revolutionizing your framing with dynamic zooms and custom start/end points.

  • Grow Mode: Advanced engine integrating with native Premiere curves for world-class easing.
  • Scroll to Zoom: Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom exactly where you point in the preview interface.
  • Transform Mode: Includes secondary mode for basic adjustments.

LuckyCut

Rapid Splicing utilizing dB-based silence detection.

  • Lightning Fast: Cut hours of silence in seconds.
  • Low CPU Overhead: Avoid the heavy resource usage of AI-transcription.

LuckyPod

Multicam Switcher perfect for automating podcast and interview edits.

  • Audio Analysis: Analyzes audio signals to automatically cut and switch cameras.
  • One-Click Assembly: Generates a complete multicam sequence instantly.

LuckyNotes

Intelligent Timeline to keep your workflow organized with a visual task system.

  • Timecode Detection: Click any timestamp to move the playhead to that moment.
  • Rich Text: Extensive text customization and direct image paste support.
  • Note: Does not create markers; solely for playhead navigation.

LuckyCurves

Perfect Dynamics giving you absolute control over animation dynamics.

  • Laser-Focused: Apply, clear, and save custom profiles with a single click.
  • Simplicity: Built purely to achieve smooth animations without complex options.

LuckyAnchor

Surgical Control for mathematical repositioning of anchor points.

  • 25-Point Alignment: Instantly reposition your clip's anchor with precision grids.
  • Custom Offsets & Shuffle: Advanced placement tools that update in real-time.
  • Note: Focuses entirely on pixel-perfect alignment without alpha channel detection.
