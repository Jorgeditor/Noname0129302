Frequently Asked Questions

How do I deactivate my license?

To deactivate your license, please visit your LemonSqueezy portal, navigate to the licenses tab, and click 'Deactivate' next to the active machine you wish to remove.

I can't activate my product?!

If you're having trouble activating, ensure your Premiere Pro is updated to v26 or higher, check your network connection, and make sure you have available seats on your key. You can also try resetting the plugin from the extensions menu.

How many seats does my key have?

Each LuckyTools license key grants you access on up to two machines (e.g., your studio desktop and your travel laptop) simultaneously.
