Clarifications & Requirements Must Read

Which languages are supported by LuckyTools?

LuckyTools is **exclusively compatible with Adobe Premiere Pro in English or Spanish**. We have prioritized these two languages because they are the most widely used among editors. Supporting additional languages is an extremely heavy task, so we haven't implemented it yet. To ensure the plugin functions correctly, please make sure your Premiere Pro installation is set to either English or Spanish.

Is LuckyTools compatible with macOS?

Currently, LuckyTools is exclusively available for Windows. I do not support macOS at this time. If you are a Mac user, I kindly ask that you refrain from purchasing the plugin, as it will not function on your system. I hope to explore Mac support in the future, but for now, this is a Windows-only toolkit.

Which versions of Adobe Premiere Pro are supported?

I guarantee 100% stable performance only for Adobe Premiere Pro 2026 (v26.0) or higher. While the tools may load on versions below v26, they are prone to significant bugs and instability. Please be advised that I do not offer refunds for issues resulting from use on unsupported legacy versions. I strongly recommend updating your software before purchase.

Does it work perfectly on Windows with Premiere 2026?

Yes! LuckyTools is natively built and optimized for Windows environments. When paired with Premiere Pro 2026, you will experience high feature compatibility, good stability, and a smooth editing workflow.

Are there any bugs or known limitations in the plugin?

Transparency is key: LuckyTools was built from the ground up with AI assistance. While it is an optimized and stable tool, it’s important to understand that it may not be as polished as software developed by large teams over many years. Because it was created with the help of AI, you might run into some quirks or minor bugs that wouldn’t be there in massive corporate tools.

This is an evolving project. Please keep this in mind before purchasing; your support directly funds the ongoing updates and fixes that make these tools better for every editor in the community!

Does this plugin replace other dedicated tools?

It does not intend to. The focus with LuckyTools is to group everyday functions to speed up workflows quickly and easily. Because of this, the tools are straightforward in terms of configuration and depth—the goal is to maintain ease of use without overwhelming settings or complex menus.

LuckyTools values the hard work and dedication of the developers behind established tools and does not seek to replace them. Their dedicated plugins are optimized and comprehensive. LuckyTools is a tool developed for editors, by editors, aiming to make the job just a little bit easier. If highly advanced features are required, those specialized alternatives remain the best option.

Ultimately, the true vision behind LuckyTools is to act as a unified ecosystem, where all its panels and tools communicate seamlessly with each other to multiply your efficiency across the entire editing process.

Included Panels

Workflow • Speed

LuckySearch:
Your Central Hub

Access everything from one place. Find tools, effects, presets, and more instantly. LuckySearch appears and disappears on demand, keeping your workspace clean and fully connected to all your shortcuts.

Make it truly yours with extensive customization options. Change colors, pick your favorite typography, or even rename the search bar to match your personal vibe. With thousands of possible combinations, you can tailor everything to feel perfectly aligned with your workflow.

Boost your efficiency with dynamic shortcut buttons that automatically surface your most recently used effects for instant re-application. You can also curate your own selection of favorites, pinning them directly to the hub for immediate, one-click access to your most essential tools.

Zero-Lag Performance: Experience real-time preset reading with absolutely zero lag. Forget about loading times when searching or applying your saved effects. Your tools are always ready the moment you need them.
Critical Note on Presets: Please be aware that due to inherent Adobe API limitations, custom interpolation curves CANNOT be preserved when presets are programmatically applied. This is a platform-wide constraint. Depending on the duration or the type of animation, some results will look extremely different, requiring manual adjustments to your easing handles for precise timing.
Control • Power

LuckyShortcuts:
Turbo Hotkeys

Assign individual actions inside LuckyTools directly to your keyboard. Move faster with zero latency when applying presets, transitions, or effects to your clips.

The system is engineered exclusively for lightning-fast, 1-to-1 shortcuts to essential actions. Complex multi-effect macros or chaining functions are not supported; the architecture is dedicated to guaranteeing absolute stability and instant execution for individual commands.

Assets • Management

LuckyOrganize:
Structure Folders

Keep your project bins perfectly organized. Instantly generate folder structures or auto-sort your media and sequences based on predefined logical patterns.

Please note: LuckyOrganize exclusively manages items within your Premiere Project panel; it does not move external files on your hard drive, nor does it import media directly into your timeline. The configuration is intentionally basic—just set a folder, name, color, and text filter. No overwhelming settings or complex menus, just a straightforward organizer designed to make your workspace look and feel beautifully structured.

Focus • Speed

LuckyZoom:
Surgical Framing

Revolutionize your framing with dynamic zooms that allow you to define custom start and end points for any shot. Whether you need a smooth zoom-in, a strategic zoom-out, or a point-A-to-B shift, LuckyZoom delivers perfect results every time—including dynamic static framing.

The panel is powered by the ultimate Grow mode, an advanced engine that integrates directly with native Premiere handles and curves to deliver world-class easing and unparalleled surgical precision. A secondary Transform mode is also included for basic adjustments.

Navigate your workspace with a familiar, intuitive preview interface. Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom exactly where you're pointing, just like the native Premiere Pro experience, making precision adjustments and frame inspection faster and more natural than ever.

Editing • Speed

LuckyCut:
Rapid Splicing

Our dB-based silence detection is lightyears faster than transcription-based tools. Cut hours of silence in seconds without the heavy CPU overhead of AI-transcription, keeping your timeline fluid and lightweight.

Audio • Multicam

LuckyPod:
Multicam Switcher

Automate your podcast and interview edits. LuckyPod analyzes audio signals to automatically cut and switch between cameras, generating a complete multicam assembly in a single click.

Workflow • Organization

LuckyNotes:
Intelligent Timeline

Keep your workflow organized in Premiere. LuckyNotes integrates a visual task system right in your editing panel so you never miss an important adjustment. Enjoy extensive text customization and the ability to seamlessly copy and paste images directly into your notes.

Enhance your navigation with automatic timecode detection. Simply click any timestamp in your notes to instantly move your playhead to that exact moment in the sequence. Please note: LuckyNotes does not create markers; it is designed to move your playhead for instant navigation.

Animation • Precision

LuckyCurves:
Perfect Dynamics

Take absolute control over your animation dynamics. Please note that LuckyCurves serves a highly specific purpose: applying curves, clearing curves, and creating custom profiles. There are no other complex options—it is a laser-focused tool designed purely to achieve smooth animations.

Precision • Layout

LuckyAnchor:
Surgical Control

Precision is key to great editing. Reposition the anchor point of your clips with mathematical exactness, saving minutes of frustrating manual adjustments.

Please note: LuckyAnchor is a specialized layout tool designed solely for the mathematical repositioning of anchor points. It does not feature alpha channel detection; its focus is entirely on providing instant, pixel-perfect alignment.
