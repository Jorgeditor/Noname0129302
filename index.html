Compatibility & Requirements

Is LuckyTools compatible with macOS?

Currently, LuckyTools is exclusively available for Windows. I do not support macOS at this time. If you are a Mac user, please refrain from purchasing the plugin, as it will not function on your system. I hope to explore Mac support in the future, but for now, this is a Windows-only toolkit.

Which versions of Adobe Premiere Pro are supported?

I guarantee 100% stable performance only for Adobe Premiere Pro 2026 (v26.0) or higher. While the tools may load on versions below v26, they are prone to significant bugs and instability. Please be advised that I do not offer refunds for issues resulting from use on unsupported legacy versions. I strongly recommend updating your software before purchase.

Does it work perfectly on Windows with Premiere 2026?

Yes! LuckyTools is natively built and optimized for Windows environments. When paired with Premiere Pro 2026, you will experience full feature compatibility, maximum stability, and the smoothest possible editing workflow.
